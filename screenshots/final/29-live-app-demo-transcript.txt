Demo summary:
  • auth-service issues JWT against Postgres-backed local users
  • anonymousId comes from identity-service via Resilience4j CB
  • gateway-service validates JWT signature/exp + checks Redis for health status
  • GREEN ⇒ entry allowed, RED ⇒ entry denied (either invalid token OR risk flag)
